Product Design

For my product designs, I have explored various ways of designing a product, either through paper prototypes or on Rhino. It has been a journey to create these products, but it was great to see the final versions.

Lamp Design

Here are the views of my lamp design. I folded pieces of paper and the cut them towards the middle, forming triangles. With those triangle flaps, I brought them together to the middle to create a floral bulb shape. I combined all the flower bulbs together using glue, creating this lamp shape. I think this lamp piece will be a success since it can be put anywhere someone want to put it. It's very decorative, yet minimalistic.

Mowom

Mowom is a kitchen utensil for users to cut food easily with one or both hands. With the use of a roll cutter and a container to hold the food after cutting, the users can cut their food much easier.

These are the intitial sketches I had when coming up with ideas on what to make in correlations with different excursions, such as weather or random words.

After lots of rendering on the Rhino software, this is the final product with different view perspectives to give users a sense of how the object will work.
