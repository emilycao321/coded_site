About Me

I’m a designer, who loves traveling and photography!

I am from New York, NY and I am currently attending the University of Michigan- Ann Arbor with a BFA degree, focusing on UX Design and Interaction Design. When I am not researcing about users interactions with products and designing for a better interaction, I like to travel, take photos, and drink boba! I love food and just a little tip, definitely go to Burger & Lobester (NYC) during Restaurant Week. Great price for the food that you get!

Not to mention, I enjoy cooking because it makes me feel good that I know someone is enjoying the food that I made. Something that takes a while to prepare, but at the end, is worth the effort put into it. I also love jewelry-making, especially earrings because I get to make fashion statements that brings out the creativity in me in the form of an object that can be worn for a long time.
