Video

Filmmaking is one of my passion. I tend to go towards the vlog route, rather than the big production form of filmmaking. Below are some videos I have made in the past and I hope you enjoy them!

52 Whalien

In a big world, you are 1 out of 8 billion people. There can be times when you feel isolated, whether it is because of your personality or a certain illness. It is difficult to feel like you are part of something when the world seems to turn against you. With 52 Whalien, a fictional story about a real whale called 52 Blue, who is isolated because of its frequency, 52 Blue can be your source of motivation in life.

Last Martha's Vineyard

Every year, RTK goes to Martha's Vineyard as a little getaway while still practicing to show the board members what they've got. My last Martha's Vineyard was on the year 2018 and I wanted to film my last time with RTK at Martha's Vineyard.

Time Traveler

Brian Lee, an incoming artist from CA, produced a song called Time Traveler. At NYU's Summer Filmmaking Program, Brian, me, and our other team members created a short music video on how people should pursue their dreams, even while life gets tough.
