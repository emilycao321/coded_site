Rosie's Theater Kids

During my internship with Rosie's Theater Kids, I created various design and social media content to be uploaded on their social media accounts. I led the campaign for 2020's Passing It On: RISE UP. Below are a few social media content that I designed.

To see other posts that I designed, check out Rosie's Theater Kids's Instagram account:

Alum Profile Posts

The Gala camapign was about rising up and to showcase the term "rise up," I have asked alums about how they are doing in life and how RTKids have helped them in their career. Above is one of the many post on Rosie's Theater Kids's Instagram account of one of their alums: Zi Ying Cao.

With each alum post, there will be a donation post to remind the audience that donation given to RTKids will help support the students to achieving much more in the future.
