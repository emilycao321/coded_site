SLIT & SLIP
This lamp is an
exploration of curves
and flow with the
technique of slit and
slip, which a paper will
be cut in the middle and
another section of
paper will be slipped
inside the slit. With the
use of the light source,
the form of the the
FINAL
curves of the paper
shows dimensions
through its shadows
and the light from the
light source.
