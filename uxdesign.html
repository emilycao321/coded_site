Spotify Alarm Clock

I have used Spotify for a long time and one feature that I've always felt was missing is an alarm feature that helps me wake up. Giving users the opportunity to choose to use the feature will give users the freedom to choose what they want to do with the alarm feature. Implementing the feature will be a good offer for Spotify customers.
