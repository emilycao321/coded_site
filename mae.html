MA:E Magazine

MA:E Magazine is an interest-based publication and creative collective created in the University of Michigan by and for the Asian Pacific Islander Desi American (APIDA) community, based in Ann Arbor, Michigan.

Emerge Designs

Designed magazine layouts for MA:E's November 2020 Emerge issue. With the use of Procreate, I illustrated line art of Lulu Wang's movie, The Farewell. To put the content together, I used InDesign to design the spread of APIDA in Hollywood, the two pages after the front cover, and the credits page.

To see the full magazine, check out: maemag.com
